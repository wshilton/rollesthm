Rolle's Theorem

Carefully read the following theorem. Click on to hear the mathematical statement interpreted verbally, as needed. Refer to Addendum 2 in the handout.


Let $a,b\in\mathbb{R}$ and let $f:[a,b]\to\mathbb{R}$ be continuously differentiable. If

$f(a)=f(b)$,

then there exists $\xi\in(a,b)$ such that

$f(b)-f(a)=f_x(\xi)(b-a).$
